PS: "Show, don't tell" is a piece of advice that every amateur writer receives. The point is to show actions and emotions rather than tell how a character is feeling. Then, let the readers interpret what they see.

"Carlos sat and cried when he received the letter" > "Carlos feels terrible when he realizes Yuliana didn't love him"
